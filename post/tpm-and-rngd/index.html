tcsd and rngd together

Jun 4, 2016 · 1 minute read
englishlinuxknow-how

There’s a tricky way to don’t choose between rngd or SSH keys wired with your computer because it is possible to use it in one time, despite the fact that they both want exclusive access to TPM.

To do that, you should create a file 

/etc/modules-load.d/tpm-rng.conf
with one line: 
tpm-rng
And restart or modprobe that module. When you can see a /dev/hwrng device, then it’s necessary to point a rngd daemon to it(f.e. in /etc/modules-load.d/tpm-rng.conf on Archlinux): 
RNGD_OPTS="-r /dev/hwrng"

And that’s all. Both services are running on my laptop without any problem.
